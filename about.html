About Us

Our Story

Scapegoats Snowballs was founded in 2010 by Maria Johnson, who wanted to bring the joy of authentic, gourmet snowballs to our community. What started as a small cart at local events has grown into the beloved neighborhood spot that it is today.

We take pride in our craft, using only the finest ingredients and house-made syrups to create unique flavor combinations that you won't find anywhere else. Our snowballs are known for their fluffy texture and rich flavors, creating the perfect treat for hot summer days or anytime you want to add a little sweetness to your life.

Our Values

Quality First

We use only premium syrups and the fluffiest shaved ice for the perfect texture and flavor.

Community Focus

We're proud to be a gathering place for families and friends in our community.

Sustainability

We use eco-friendly packaging and support local suppliers whenever possible.
