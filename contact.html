Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer dairy-free options?

Yes! Most of our fruit flavors are dairy-free. We also have special options for different dietary needs.

How far in advance should I place a large order?

For orders of 20+ snowballs, we recommend 48 hours notice to ensure we can accommodate your request.

Can I request a custom flavor?

Absolutely! We love creating custom flavors. Contact us with your idea and we'll do our best to make it happen.

Can I bring my own container (BYOC) for snowballs?

Yes! Our EcoBoost pricing applies to clean containers clearly marked with ounce measurements. Save money and help the environment!

Is there a minimum order for catering?

We recommend a minimum of 20 snowballs for catering orders to ensure we can provide the best service and variety.

Are pets allowed at your location?

Unfortunately, we cannot accommodate dogs due to our livestock guardian dogs, who may view new dogs as threats or playmates.
